Hello

My name is Kasper Kain. I'm an aspiring software developer based in Orlando Florida and student at Thinkful. I have modest experience in developing games and mobile applications as a hobby and now strive to expand my skill set to include web development as well. I'm proficient at using a wide group of tools such as Game Engines and frameworks, Art Programs, DAWS and several miscellaneous programming tools. My programming languages of choice are C#, Java and JavaScript. I also produce digital music, create 3D Models and draw. My ultimate goal in life is to make a career out of doing what I already do for fun > Solve problems and create things.

Where I come from

I was born and raised along the coast of Alabama. If you're thinking trailer parks and churches, You're (sort of) correct! Where many would see this as a disadvantage however, I would disagree. I come from a line of hard-working and dedicated folks who have taught me that anything is achievable with enough blood and sweat. I am who I am because of this. My skills and talents aren't a product of universities or social pressure, But through years of sheer dedication, self-discipline and sacrifice, And that is my advantage. I strive to excel at everything I do. Failure simply isn't an option. My motto - "Whatever you do, Do it well".

How I got here

Creativity and problem solving has always been a core part of my nature. Growing up I would spend many days out of any given week submerged in some sort of personal project. Rather that be solving puzzles, building puzzles, breaking and fixing electronics, converting PCs into dedicated arcade machines, or simply taking things apart just to see how they work. Fast forward to adulthood and I have combined this aspect of my nature with my newfound passion for writing and designing software. I am the same as I was then, But now with the power to create and manipulate a digital world. Programming is my passion and lifestyle. I do what I do not for the sake of a paycheck, but because I can't imagine doing anything else.
